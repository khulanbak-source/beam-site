Who this is for

Sales reps

  • 😩 Logging feels like a second job. After a full day of meetings, filling in CRM fields is the last thing anyone wants to do.
  • 🕐 Details go missing. By the time the rep sits down to log, half the conversation is already forgotten.
  • 📋 Too many fields. Name, status, source, product, notes, next step — each entry takes 5 to 10 minutes to do properly.
  • 📱 The tool is wrong for the job. Reps live on their phone and in messaging apps, not inside a CRM dashboard.

Sales managers

  • 🔁 Constantly chasing updates. "Did you log this?" is a daily conversation that shouldn't need to happen.
  • 📉 No visibility into what is working. Without consistent logs, there is no way to see which channels, products, or approaches are converting.
  • 🤷 Hard to coach. If there is no record of what was said or what the next step is, there is nothing to review or improve.
  • 🔥 Hot leads go cold. Without a clear next action logged somewhere visible, follow-ups slip through the cracks.
How it works
01
💬
Rep sends a message
Voice note or text in Telegram, right after a meeting. No app to open, no form to fill.
02
🧠
Beam extracts the details
Name, status, product interest, and next action are pulled out automatically. If something is missing, Beam asks once.
03
📋
CRM is updated
New leads are created. Existing ones are updated. No duplicate entries, no manual data entry.
04
📅
Next action is tracked
When a rep confirms a follow-up time, a Google Calendar event is created. A daily Telegram digest keeps upcoming actions visible.

Everything captured.
Nothing to fill in.

Reps send a message the same way they would text a colleague. Beam handles the structure, the CRM entry, and the follow-up tracking.

  • Voice and text input in Mongolian via Chimege STT
  • Recognizes returning leads and prevents duplicate records
  • Google Calendar event created when a next action time is confirmed
  • Daily Telegram digest of upcoming follow-ups for each rep
  • Ask Beam about any lead by name and get an instant answer
  • Weekly summary report sent to management automatically
